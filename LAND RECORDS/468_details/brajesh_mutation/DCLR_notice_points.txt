
1) ye ki kharika gaon me khata 313 aur khesra 468 jamen 8 khaata 1 dhoor hamare purvaj shri Ram lagan singh ke name se rasid saalo se kat raha hai aur humara dakhil kabja bhi hai jamin par.
2) humare plot ke purab taraf shir rasendra singh pita ramdev singh ji ka makan hai. jinka gramin raste se apne ghar tak jane ka koi rasta nahi tha to humare jamin se hokar aate jate the.
3) vars 2016 me ram lagan singh ke 5 putro me jamin ka baitwara hua sabhi gaon walo ke samne. Is baitware me aapase sahmate se ye tay hua ke 9 feet ka rasta jamin ke utar taraf se Ramdev singh ke gharwalo ke aane jane ke liye aur pacho farik aapas me aane jane ke liye chorenge. Sare gaon wale sahmat the.
4) pechle varsh 2022 august me jab pacho log apna baitware wale jamin aur ghar me boundary etc karne aaye to ramdev singh ke gharwalo ne virodh kiya aur kaha ke unhe beech se 10 feet ka rasta chahiye aur 2016 me kiye 9 feet ke raste par raje nahi hue.
5) Ek baar phir pacho fariko ne evam samast gaon walo ke sahmate se 10 feet ka rasta khale gamin ke beech se diya gaya.
6) par sabhi faisala hone ke baad ramdev singh ke parijano ne farik dastawej CO office me jama kiya jisme likha tha ke ramlagan singh ne 1968 me 1500 sq feet jamin ramdev singh ko likh de the jo ke bilkul fariji hai. 
7) humare dono bar ke baitware 2016 aur 2022 ke baitware me ramdev singh ke ghar wale bhi samil the jinhone ne aane jane ka raste chorne ka anurodh kiya jo humne sarpanch ke majudgi me evam sabhi gaon wale ke parmukh ke signature se 10 feet ka raste apne upyog ke liye aur ramdeev singh ke parijano ke aane jane ke liye chora tha.
